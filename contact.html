Quick answers
Can I request a new platform?

Yes — email with the platform name and why you'd use it. Apple Music is the most requested and being looked into.

A song isn't matching correctly

Matching uses the iTunes Search API. Try the format Artist – Song Title for better results. Still wrong? Email us with the track details.

Can I use PlaylistBridge in my project?

Reach out via email with what you have in mind. Collaborations and integrations are welcome — let's talk.
