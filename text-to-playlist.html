How it works

Paste. Pick a platform. Done.

1

Copy your text list from anywhere

Grab a list from a message, Reddit post, website, note app — anywhere. The format doesn't matter at all. Don't clean it up, just copy the raw text.

Any source, any format
2

Paste it into PlaylistBridge

Drop the raw text into the input box. Our parser automatically removes numbered prefixes, bullets, dashes, and any extra formatting. Up to 50 tracks per session.

Auto-parses messy formatting
3

Choose Spotify, YouTube Music, or YouTube

Select your preferred music platform. PlaylistBridge fetches metadata for each track using iTunes and generates direct search links — one click per song, no manual typing.
