    Questions

    Can I convert a ChatGPT playlist to Spotify or YouTube Music?

    Yes. Paste your AI-generated playlist and instantly get platform-ready links. No login or account is required.

    Does this create a real playlist?

    PlaylistBridge generates instant listening links and may offer a temporary YouTube queue. It does not save playlists permanently to your account.

    Do I need to sign up?

    No. The app works entirely without logins, accounts, or saving your personal data.

    Which music platforms are supported?

    Currently, we generate search links for Spotify, YouTube Music, and standard YouTube.

    How does InstantPlay work?

    InstantPlay finds your song on YouTube and plays it directly on this page using YouTube's official embed. No audio is hosted by PlaylistBridge.

    Is InstantPlay free to use?

    Yes, completely free. No account needed.

    Why did a song get skipped?

    Some songs may be unavailable on YouTube in your region or have been removed. InstantPlay automatically skips these and moves to the next track.

    Does InstantPlay work on mobile?

    Yes. Background play is limited on mobile browsers — install PlaylistBridge to your home screen for uninterrupted listening.

    Can I watch music videos while listening?

    Yes. InstantPlay includes a Video mode that plays the official YouTube music video directly inside the player.
