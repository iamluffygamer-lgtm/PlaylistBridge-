Why use ChatGPT for Music?

If you're using ChatGPT to discover new music, you already know it acts as a fantastic, highly-personalized DJ. Whether you asked ChatGPT for an "upbeat coding mix" or "90s underground hip-hop," it can generate incredible tracklists. But manually searching for every single song it suggests on Spotify is a massive waste of time.

How to Convert Your ChatGPT List

  • Step 1: Prompt ChatGPT. Ask it for a list of songs. For the cleanest results, simply ask it to format the output as "Artist - Song Title".
  • Step 2: Copy the Text. Highlight the text output and copy it to your clipboard. You don't need to worry about manually deleting the numbers or bullet points.
  • Step 3: Paste & Convert. Paste the raw text into PlaylistBridge. Our tool automatically cleans the formatting, fetches the metadata, and generates playable Spotify links instantly.
