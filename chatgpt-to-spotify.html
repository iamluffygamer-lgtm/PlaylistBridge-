How it works

Three steps from ChatGPT to Spotify

1

Prompt ChatGPT for a playlist

Ask ChatGPT for any style of tracklist. For the cleanest output, ask it to format results as "Artist – Song Title", but it's not required.

Works with GPT-4, GPT-4o & all versions
2

Copy the raw text output

Don't bother cleaning it up. PlaylistBridge automatically strips numbered lists, bullet points, asterisks, and ChatGPT's conversational filler text.

Auto-cleaned — no manual editing
3

Paste → select Spotify → get links

Paste into PlaylistBridge, choose Spotify, and hit Get Links. Each song is matched via metadata lookup and turned into a direct Spotify search link instantly.

No login. No account. Instant.
