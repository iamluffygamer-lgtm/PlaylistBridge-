AI Makes the List, We Make the Links

AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity have revolutionized how we discover music. You can ask them to curate highly specific vibes—like "1970s Japanese City Pop for driving at night"—and they will instantly output a perfect 15-song tracklist. But an AI's job ends at the text screen.

How to use the AI Playlist Converter

  • Copy the AI Output: Highlight the block of text the AI generated. Don't worry about conversational filler like "Here is your requested playlist:"—our tool ignores it.
  • Paste it into PlaylistBridge: Drop the text into our main converter box.
  • Choose Your Platform: Select Spotify, YouTube Music, or YouTube, and hit generate. We will instantly fetch the metadata and provide you with one-click listening links.
